About YOLO Factchecker

This tool analyzes videos using:

  • YOLOv8 object detection to identify visual elements
  • Whisper speech recognition to transcribe audio
  • Local LLM (Ollama) to analyze and fact-check content

The processing happens entirely on your local machine, with no data sent to external servers.
